Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2024

TimberLeap , operated and owned by Cosmic Rays LLC, is committed to protecting and respecting the privacy of all its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we use any personal data collected from you through our website i.e., under the operations of TimberLeap . We advise you to read this Privacy Policy carefully and make sure you understand it.

I. Data Collection

When you apply to use the services of TimberLeap we collect certain necessary data about you, and your business, which primarily include:

  • Name: To personalize your experience and communications with us.
  • Email Address: To communicate with you about service updates, notifications, and other relevant information.
  • Phone Number: For better communication in case we can 't reach out to you via other communication means.
  • Your business information (e.g. website, social media links, etc): This allows us to evaluate your business and tailor our services to your unique profile.
  • Your business address/addresses: We may ask for the business address if we proceed to write contracts on behalf of your business.

II. Storage, Use, And Security of Data

The collected data is stored in our database for the key purpose of building business partnerships. After we close any partnership, we delete all of your information except if you agree to stay connected with us for future opportunities.

Payment details are handled and hosted exclusively by Stripe, inc , which governs the handling of this data according to its own Privacy Policy. We urge you to check their privacy policy.

We may store a cookie session in your browser to improve your user experience with TimberLeap .

III. Data Used For Communication

Your email address, and your phone number are used to communicate essential information such as payment links and details, updates about your onboarding process, and other necessary service-related information.

V. Data Sharing

As service providers, TimberLeap and Cosmic Rays LLC do not share any of your personal data with third parties. However, we may share certain information, determined by you, with potential business partners.

VI. Your Data Protection Rights

Under data protection law, you have rights including your right to access, correct, erase, restrict, and object to the processing of your personal data. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the contact details provided on our website.

VII. Changes To The Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy as necessary, for instance, to comply with changes in laws and regulation or changes in our practices and procedures. Any changes will be duly made known on this platform.

VIII. Minor 's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by minors under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors under 18.

IX. Third-Party Links

We are not responsible for the privacy policies, practices, or content of any external websites or services. We encourage users to read the privacy policies of any third-party websites or services they may visit.

X. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@timberleap.com

By using our services, you consent to the terms of this privacy policy. If at any point you do not agree with the terms outlined in this privacy policy, refrain from using our services.
